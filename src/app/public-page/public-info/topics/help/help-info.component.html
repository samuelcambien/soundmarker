How can
we help you?

We hope you can find your answers in
our frequently asked questions.

You can use the Soundmarker website to upload and transfer tracks from a computer or mobile device. Press the music note icon to select up to 20 tracks from your computer or phone. Desktop users can also just drag and drop. Supported audio formats below.

We currently support following formats:

AIFF, WAVE (.WAV), FLAC, ALAC, MP3, WavPack, AAC, M4A.

If you have uploaded a supported lossy audio file format, the track will be streamed in that specific format. In the case that you’ve uploaded a lossless format, the track will be streamed in 192 kbps MP3, converted by the audiofile LAME MP3 encoder. If files are available for download, it will consist of the files as uploaded. Register for Soundmarker Pro to send lossless streams to your clients.

Your files are deleted from the Soundmarker servers after expiration.

Comments will remain accessible for one month after the expiration date of the audio files. After this date they will be removed from our servers. Try Soundmarker Pro if you need to access comments for longer periods.

Send an email to support@soundmarker.com from the same email address you used for uploading the files along with the URL of the files you’d like to remove and we’ll remove them for you. If you also want the comments removed, let us know.

Your contact information is deleted after link expiration. We have no intention to use that info for marketing or research purposes, for example, so we make sure it’s removed.

We only keep statistics of uploaded tracks for Soundmarker Pro users. Try Soundmarker Pro if you’d like more insight regarding track statistics.
